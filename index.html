From the ICU to the boardroom — and back to the truth
The Origin

The woman who had everything — and still felt like nothing.

I grew up watching two men love me differently. My daddy measured love in distance — always leaving. My papa measured it in presence — always there. By the time I was old enough to understand the difference, I had already learned to perform for love instead of receive it.

"I didn't leave the bedside. I just followed my patients to where the real damage was being done — long before they needed the ICU."

That pattern followed me from the ICU to the boardroom. From nursing to healthcare informatics. From deployment after deployment across 78 hospitals — learning to read systems, fix broken workflows, and optimize what wasn't working. I was exceptional at fixing everything except myself. The body keeps a record of what the mind refuses to process. I know this now — because I lived it, and because I reversed it.

Then I wrote the book. Not to teach — to survive. And in writing it, I found the framework that changed everything.

The Frameworks

Two frameworks. One truth.

Most people think their biggest problem is the challenge they're facing. It's not. It's that nobody ever taught them how to understand how their PAST is living inside them right now — or how to effectively DEAL with it.

Framework One

The PAST Framework™

"You're not living in the past. Your past is living in you."

P
The Pairing
Wounds Recognize Wounds
Why we magnetize what we haven't healed

Our unhealed wounds act as a signal — attracting people and situations that mirror what we haven't yet resolved within ourselves.

A
The Audition
Performing for Love
Instead of receiving it

When love was conditional in childhood, we learn to audition. To earn. To perform. The work is learning to receive love without a performance first.

S
The Squeeze
Pressure Reveals Truth
What you carry is what pours out

Under pressure, we don't rise to our intentions — we fall to our patterns. The Squeeze reveals exactly what's still unresolved inside us.

T
The Tank
What You Carry
Is what you pour

You cannot pour from an empty tank — but most high-achievers are running on fumes, giving to everyone while silently depleting themselves.

Framework Two

The DEAL Framework™

A resilience model for navigating adversity without losing yourself

D
Don't Resist Reality
Accept What Is

Fighting reality drains the energy you need to move forward. The first step is accepting the problem exists — then focusing on what you can influence.

E
Embrace Discomfort
Growth Lives Here

Discomfort isn't a sign you're doing life wrong. It's often a sign you're growing. Build the capacity to tolerate it instead of numbing, avoiding, or overworking.

A
Adjust Your Perspective
Reframe Everything

The meaning you assign shapes how you experience it. Shift from "why is this happening to me?" to "what is this teaching me?" — and watch everything change.

L
Level Up
Challenges Refine You

Challenges aren't here to define you. They're here to refine you. Every obstacle contains an opportunity to develop deeper wisdom and stronger character.
